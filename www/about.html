The story so far

I am a successful entrepreneur, experienced consultant, published author and dynamic speaker with a proven track record of building and growing businesses across multiple industries.

Who I am

As a business catalyst, my objective is to enable individuals to connect with their deeper spiritual and personal selves and integrate these elements into their ventures, resulting in companies that not only drive positive impact and results, but also align with their personal values and sense of purpose.

Having amassed decades of experience as a CEO in both Silicon Valley and New York, I possess a keen understanding of the critical role technology plays in driving business success. In today's digital-first landscape, businesses that neglect to adopt a technology-first mindset risk falling behind. As such, I firmly believe that every CEO must become a "technology CEO" to harness the leverage provided by the latest digital tools and trends.

My expertise spans all aspects of companies, from early stage startups to enterprises. Over the years, I have worked with prominent companies and startups to create strategies and products that have reached billions of people, empowered executives and employees, and improved lives.

In the realm of business, the personal aspect holds immense significance, underscoring the fact that people's individualities and identities truly matter. Business is a highly personal endeavor, where the values, beliefs, and personalities of individuals play a significant role in shaping the outcomes. As such, it is imperative to recognize the importance of human connections and to acknowledge that people are at the heart of any successful business.

Looking ahead, the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence is poised to accelerate the pace of digital transformation, presenting significant challenges for companies seeking to remain relevant. As a business catalyst, I collaborate with companies of all sizes to develop tailored digital-first strategies that address their unique needs and objectives. By fostering a culture of innovation and experimentation, I aim to help companies thrive in the digital age.
