Northstar

All business is intensely personal, therefore we as people truly and deeply matter.

The core of what I am about

I am a business catalyst. I help people to find themselves, connect with their deeper spiritual and personal selves, and then incorporate that into companies that work for their founders, have appropriate scale, and deliver meaningful impact.

You can do work that you love, under the circumstances you want to do it, in a way that not only creates generational wealth but benefits the world. The future is a world where the majority of people wake up every day inspired, feel safe in their lives, and lay their head down on their pillows at the end the day fulfilled by the work that they do and wake up the next day feeling good and not worried about their future. And the best way to do that is to work together to make it happen.

We are here to inspire people to do the things that inspire them so that, together, each of us can change our world for the better.

Intentionality and bigness, collaborative. You’re power is in the present. You can’t know the future, and even if you could, it offers no protection to do so. Being completely present, and following our inner guidance, is all the protection anyone ever needs.
